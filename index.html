About Us

We are a professional footwear sourcing and export company based in Jinjiang, Quanzhou, China. We focus on the full supply chain from product selection to final delivery.

Our work includes:

  • Style selection & product development
  • Material & color recommendation based on market trends
  • Factory sourcing & production coordination
  • Production process control & QC management
  • On-time bulk shipment delivery

We work with factories in China and Southeast Asia (including Bangladesh and others), and also support semi-finished footwear sourcing solutions.

Jinjiang Footwear Supply Chain Advantage

Located in Jinjiang – China’s global footwear manufacturing hub. We can quickly access hundreds of factories and material suppliers, ensuring fast development and flexible production capacity.
